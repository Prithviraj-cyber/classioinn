Accommodation

Hotel Classio inn features 42 grand rooms, which have been segregated into 5 categories. Each room is furnished with state-of-the-art amenities to provide you comfort at competitive rates.

DELUXE ROOM

Our hotel features 14 deluxe rooms. Plush interiors adorn the modern décor of these rooms. Affordably priced, each of these rooms comprises of chic upholstery, a king size bed with fresh linens, soft lights, a mini bar, and a tea & coffee maker.

SUPER DELUXE ROOM

We have 12 elegantly crafted Super Deluxe Rooms, which have been tastefully furnished with comfortable upholstery, including a sofa and a king size bed. They also have corner rooms with amenities like a mini bar and a tea & coffee maker.

EXECUTIVE SUITE

Ideally suited for business travellers, this room is furnished with an array of facilities like a king size bed with a separate sitting and dining area. It also has amenities like a separate work table, free fruit platter, cookies and electronic safe.

ROYAL SUITE

Representing grandeur and elegance, these rooms are replete with a regal décor. They feature a fully carpeted separate plush sitting area, a complimentary fruit basket & cookies and an electronic safe. It also has high end electronic gadgets like laptops and a work table.
