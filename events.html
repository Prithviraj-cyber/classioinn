PLAN YOUR EVENT

Hotel Classio inn features a range of venues suited for corporate & social events. Our 2 well- equipped banquet halls and board room set the tone for any kind of event. Strategically located in the heart of the city, we are adept to carve out a picture-perfect event.

BANQUET 1

Spread across an expanse of 6000 sq. ft.; Banquet Hall 1 can host a gathering of up to 1500 guests. Equipped with all the modern audio-visual facilities, this hall can be used to host corporate events like conferences, seminars and meetings.

BANQUET 2

With an area of 2500 sq. ft.; this banquet 2 features a ceiling of 8 feet. It can house up to 400 guests. It is furnished with state-of-the-art facilities and is equipped to handle numerous occasions like product launches, seminars, meetings & conferences.
