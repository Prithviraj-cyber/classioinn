DINING

Dining at Hotel Classio inn entails a culinary excellence that all delight your taste buds. Fresh ingredients, flavourful local spices, exotic herbs and inviting aromas there’s a lot going on in our delicacies. Brace yourself for an exciting dining experience at Restaurant Blu, our multi-cuisine diner, and lounge.

Restaurant Blu

Replete with an effortlessly simplistic style of cooking, this signature restaurant of our hotel brings to you a diverse spread of delicacies from an à la carte menu. Be them authentic Indian dishes curated with local ingredients and spices or global delicacies created with exotic herbs – our seasoned chefs craft each dish with equal passion. So, unwind with your folks as you indulge in lip smacking goodies from our kitchen or simply grab a quick bite as you bond with your associates over a buffet breakfast.

Bar

The perfect fusion of good food, good music and good vibes, the lounge of Hotel Classio inn is the right place to ring in good times. Treat yourself to a lavish variety of chilled mocktails, fine spirits and other refreshing drinks as you indulge in great conversations with your friends or simply relax by yourself in its soothing ambience.
